## 摘要
Ras蛋白的功能依赖于一个或多个脂质修饰。N-Ras通过DHHC棕榈酰基转移酶和ABHD17丝氨酸水解酶的去棕榈酰化动态调节，对于NRAS突变急性髓系白血病（AML）细胞的生长至关重要。在这里，我们展示了ABD778（一种体内活性的ABHD17抑制剂）选择性地降低NRAS突变AML和黑色素瘤细胞系的生长，并与MEK抑制剂PD0325901（PD901；mirdametinib）具有协同作用。机制上，ABD778和PD901诱导丝裂原活化蛋白激酶（MAPK）通路活化的深度且持久的抑制。联合治疗延长了移植NrasG12D AML的小鼠的生存期，复发时获得性旁路突变赋予耐药性并恢复MAPK激活。ABD778增强了PI3激酶、pan-Ras三复合物和FLT3抑制剂的抗白血病活性，并在FLT3抑制剂耐药的患者来源异种移植模型中恢复了gilteritinib敏感性。这些研究验证了棕榈酰化循环作为NRAS突变癌症的治疗靶点。

## Abstract
Ras proteins are functionally dependent on one or more lipid modifications. The dynamic palmitoylation of N-Ras by DHHC palmitoyl acyltransferases and depalmitoylation by ABHD17 serine hydrolases is essential for the growth of NRAS-mutant acute myeloid leukemia (AML) cells. Here we show that ABD778, an in vivo-active ABHD17 inhibitor, selectively reduces the growth of NRAS-mutant AML and melanoma cell lines and is synergistic with the MEK inhibitor PD0325901 (PD901; mirdametinib). Mechanistically, ABD778 and PD901 induce deep and durable suppression of mitogen activated protein kinase (MAPK) pathway activation. Co-treatment extended the survival of mice transplanted with NrasG12D AMLs, which acquired by-pass mutations at relapse that conferred drug resistance and restored MAPK activation. ABD778 augmented the anti-leukemia activity of PI3 kinase, pan-Ras tri-complex, and FLT3 inhibitors, and restored gilteritinib sensitivity in a patient-derived xenograft model of FLT3 inhibitor resistance. These studies validate the palmitoylation cycle as a therapeutic target in NRAS-mutant cancers.
