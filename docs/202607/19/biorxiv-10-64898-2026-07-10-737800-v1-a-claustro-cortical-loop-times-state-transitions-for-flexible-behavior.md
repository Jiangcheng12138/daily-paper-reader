## 摘要
亚稳态动力学，即神经活动在短暂稳定模式之间移动，被认为支撑着大脑认知和行为功能所依赖的实时协调1-3。在皮层中，这种状态的最简单形式是活跃的UP状态（神经元放电）与静止的DOWN状态（放电显著减少）之间的交替，这种双稳态结构塑造了传入信息的处理方式4-8。这种亚稳态动力学在皮层中普遍存在9,10，然而，其状态转换如何被计时和控制，以及这种计时如何塑造行为，仍然是未解决的问题。在这里，我们展示了在自由行为小鼠执行线索引导的切换任务时，额叶皮层的亚稳态动力学是由其与屏状核（一个体积小、连接广泛的皮层下结构）的相互回路共同塑造的，并且该回路对这些状态转换的计时对于有效的灵活行为是必要的。屏状核活动先于皮层UP到DOWN状态转换，向前扣带皮层传递一个低维驱动力，该驱动力与皮层状态切换的轴对齐。这种驱动力并非通过屏状核放电率的增加来携带，而是通过瞬时协调：在平均放电率下降的同时，群体同步性在转换时达到峰值。沉默前扣带皮层投射的屏状核末梢减少了定向的屏状核-皮层耦合，通过延长UP状态和缩短DOWN状态使皮层偏离DOWN状态，并选择性地损害线索驱动的切换，同时保留探索性觅食。这些结果将灵活行为置于一个特定的皮层下回路控制之下，该回路为皮层状态转换计时，为许多精神和神经疾病的认知僵化提供了回路水平的切入点11-13。

## Abstract
Metastable dynamics, in which neural activity moves between transiently stable patterns, have been proposed to underlie the real-time coordination on which the brains cognitive and behavioral functions depend1-3. In the cortex, the simplest form of this regime is the alternation between active  UP states, in which neurons fire, and silent  DOWN states, in which firing is strongly reduced, a bistable architecture that shapes how afferent information is processed 4-8. Such metastable dynamics are ubiquitous across cortex 9,10, yet how their state transitions are timed and controlled, and how this timing shapes behavior, remain open questions. Here we show, in freely behaving mice performing a cue-guided switching task, that the metastable dynamics of frontal cortex are jointly shaped by its reciprocal loop with the claustrum, a small and widely connected subcortical structure, and that the loops timing of these state transitions is required for efficient flexible behavior. Claustrum activity led the cortical UP-to-DOWN state transition, delivering to anterior cingulate cortex a low-dimensional drive aligned with the axis along which the cortical state switches. This drive was carried not by a rise in claustral firing but by transient coordination: population synchrony peaked at the transition even as mean rate fell. Silencing ACC-projecting claustrum terminals reduced directed claustro-cortical coupling, biased cortex away from the DOWN state by prolonging UP states and shortening DOWN states, and selectively impaired cue-driven switching while sparing exploratory foraging. These results place flexible behavior under the control of a defined cortico-subcortical loop that times cortical state transitions, offering a circuit-level entry point into the cognitive rigidity of many psychiatric and neurological conditions11-13.

- **屏状核输入对线索引导切换是必要的**：光抑制ACC投射的屏状核末梢选择性降低命中率（-16%），不改变虚报率或前端口访问率，提示特异性损害线索驱动切换，保留探索性觅食。
