## 摘要
低温，即恒温动物核心体温降至35摄氏度以下，会引发快速、异步的非自主战栗产热；然而，负责启动这一反应的下丘脑神经元尚未明确。本研究通过逆行跨突触示踪结合RNA-seq，在下丘脑背内侧核团（DMH）中鉴定出一个独特的富含Calbindin D28k（CALB1）的VGLUT2阳性神经元群体（DMHCalb1/Vglut2）。来自化学遗传学、光遗传学、光纤光度钙成像和肌电图（EMG）实验的汇聚证据表明，这些神经元选择性介导低温触发的战栗产热。双向调控DMHCalb1/Vglut2活性可调节战栗强度，而表达VGLUT2的CALB1阴性DMH神经元则负责非战栗产热（NST）。遗传消融从LSI/POA/VLPAG投射至DMHCalb1/Vglut2的传入输入，以及离体药理学突触阻断，均未能消除低温下的战栗反应，表明其激活独立于经典的冷感受回路。低温显著抑制了瞬时A型钾电流（IA），而不影响延迟整流钾电流（IK），同时伴随相对于正常体温36°C时IA失活时间常数的显著减小。总之，低温通过IA依赖性重塑动作电位动力学提高DMHCalb1/Vglut2的兴奋性。缩短的动作电位复极化和后超极化持续时间协同增强神经元放电，构成了DMHCalb1/Vglut2神经元驱动战栗启动的内在机制。

## Abstract
Hypothermia, a decline in core body temperature below 35{degrees}C in homeotherms, elicits rapid, asynchronous involuntary shivering thermogenesis; however, the hypothalamic neurons responsible for initiating this response remain uncharacterized. Here, retrograde transsynaptic tracing combined with RNA-seq identified a distinct population of VGLUT2-positive neurons enriched with Calbindin D28k (CALB1) within the dorsomedial hypothalamic nucleus (DMHCalb1/Vglut2). Convergent evidence from chemogenetic, optogenetic, fiber photometry calcium imaging, and electromyography (EMG) experiments demonstrated that these neurons selectively mediate hypothermia-triggered shivering thermogenesis. Bidirectional perturbation of DMHCalb1/Vglut2 activity modulated shivering intensity, while CALB1-negative VGLUT2-expressing DMH neurons were responsible for non-shivering thermogenesis (NST). Genetic ablation of afferent inputs from LSI/POA/VLPAG projecting to DMHCalb1/Vglut2, as well as ex vivo pharmacological synaptic blockade, failed to eliminate shivering responses during hypothermia, indicating that their activation is independent of canonical cold-responsive circuits. Hypothermia significantly suppressed transient A-type K (IA) currents, without affecting delayed rectifier K (IK) currents, accompanied by a pronounced reduction in the IA inactivation time constant relative to normothermic 36 {degrees}C. Collectively, hypothermia elevates DMHCalb1/Vglut2 excitability through IA-dependent reshaping of action potential dynamics. Shortened action potential repolarization and afterhyperpolarization duration cooperatively boost neuronal firing, constituting an intrinsic mechanism for DMHCalb1/Vglut2 neuron-driven shivering initiation.

- **核心问题**：恒温动物（如小鼠）在核心体温（Tcore）降至35°C以下的低温状态时，会触发快速的战栗产热（shivering thermogenesis）以维持体温。然而，负责启动这一关键生理反应的特异性神经元及其内在机制尚不明确。
- **研究背景**：
    - 传统的体温调节理论认为，大脑缺少内在的冷敏感神经元，体温稳态主要由下丘脑视前区（POA）的温敏神经元控制。
    - 已知下丘脑背内侧核（DMH）在体温调节中扮演重要角色，但其在低温诱发战栗产热中的具体作用和神经元亚型未被阐明。
    - 先前的研究虽然鉴定了一些与产热相关的神经元（如dmVMH、POA BRS3、DMH GABA神经元），但这些神经元主要与非战栗产热（NST）及自主神经输出有关，而非专门调控低温下的战栗。
- **研究意义**：鉴定出专门负责驱动低温战栗产热的神经元群体及其细胞内在激活机制，不仅完善了中枢体温调节的理论框架，也为临床治疗（如治疗性低温中抑制副作用性战栗）提供了潜在的精准干预靶点。
