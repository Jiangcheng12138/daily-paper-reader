## 摘要
源自藏红花的西红柿苷（Crocin）表现出抗癌特性，但其作用背后的分子通路尚未得到完全表征。在本研究中，我们采用高剂量扰动策略（1-2 mM 西红柿苷），通过时间序列转录组学探究 HepG2 肝细胞癌细胞中最大程度的通路参与情况。我们对细胞进行了 2、6、12 和 24 小时的处理，并分析了每个时间点的转录组和剪接谱。我们在每种条件下识别出 7400-12,100 个差异表达基因 (DEG)，其中高剂量 (CR2) 产生的 DEG 总数更多，但低剂量 (CR1) 表现出不同的通路优先级。在多个时间点，剪接体通路在 CR1 的下调通路中排名第一（错误发现率 FDR p = 10⁻²¹ 至 10⁻³⁶），而在 CR2 中仅排名第四，这表明通路优先级存在剂量依赖性差异。差异剪接分析揭示了功能性剪接体破坏，每种条件下观察到 2000-2600 个显著的外显子跳跃事件，以及包括 HNRNPH1 在内的剪接体组件的异常剪接（剪接包含百分比变化 dPSI = -0.78 至 -0.89）。此外，24 小时时有 66 个涉及非酒精性脂肪性肝病的基因被下调（FDR p = 8x10⁻⁸）。西红柿苷暴露持续下调剪接体机器基因，同时上调衰老和自噬通路。这些发现确定了剪接体组件和 RNA 加工机器是西红柿苷敏感通路。

## Abstract
Saffron-derived crocin exhibits anti-cancer properties, but the pathways underlying its effects remain incompletely characterized. Here, we utilized a high-dose perturbation strategy (1-2 mM crocin) to probe maximal pathway engagement in HepG2 hepatocellular carcinoma cells via time-series transcriptomics. We treated cells for 2, 6, 12, and 24 h and analyzed transcriptomic and splicing profiles at each timepoint. We identified 7400-12,100 differentially expressed genes (DEGs) per condition, with the higher dose (CR2) producing more total DEGs but the lower dose (CR1) demonstrating differential pathway prioritization. The spliceosome pathway ranked first among downregulated pathways for CR1 across multiple timepoints (false discovery rate, FDR p = 10-21 to 10-36) but only fourth for CR2, suggesting dose-dependent differences in pathway prioritization. Differential splicing analysis revealed functional spliceosome disruption, with 2000-2600 significant exon skipping events per condition and aberrant splicing of spliceosome components including HNRNPH1 (change in percent spliced in, dPSI = -0.78 to -0.89). Additionally, 66 genes implicated in non-alcoholic fatty liver disease were downregulated at 24 h (FDR p = 8x10-8). Crocin exposure consistently downregulated spliceosomal machinery genes while upregulating senescence and autophagy pathways. These findings identify spliceosome components and RNA processing machinery as crocin-sensitive pathways.
