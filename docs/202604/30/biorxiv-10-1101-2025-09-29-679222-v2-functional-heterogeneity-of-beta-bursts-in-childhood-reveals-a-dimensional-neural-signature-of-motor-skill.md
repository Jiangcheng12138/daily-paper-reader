## 摘要
beta 频段的活动在感觉运动处理中起着至关重要的作用，其瞬时爆发表现出独特的波形基序，可能反映了不同的神经计算。然而，这些动力学特征如何与运动发育及神经发育状况相关联尚不明确。本研究利用脑电图（EEG）调查了患有和不患有发育性协调障碍（DCD）儿童的感觉运动 beta 爆发。我们表征了在执行和观察粗大及精细运动动作期间的 beta 爆发特征。结果显示，传统的频谱特征和整体爆发率在组间没有差异。相比之下，DCD 儿童在特定爆发波形基序的频率调节上表现出异常，包括非典型的半球侧化和受损的半球间连接。关键的是，基序特异性爆发率与个体的运动表现呈系统性相关，且这种关联在不同组别中均有体现。这些发现表明，波形特异性的 beta 爆发动力学追踪的是运动表现的梯度变化，而非仅与诊断状态相关。因此，beta 爆发的变异性可能反映了运动表现的功能表达，而非 DCD 特有的神经机制。本研究强调了 beta 爆发波形多样性对于理解发育过程中感觉运动功能变异性的重要意义。

## Abstract
Activity in the beta frequency band appears to play a critical role in sensorimotor processing, with transient  bursts exhibiting distinct waveform motifs that may reflect different neural computations. However, how these dynamics relate to motor development and neurodevelopmental conditions remains unclear. Here, we used EEG to investigate sensorimotor beta bursts in children with and without developmental coordination disorder (DCD). We characterized beta bursts during action execution and observation of gross and fine motor actions. Conventional spectral features and overall burst rates did not differ between groups. In contrast, children with DCD showed altered rate modulation of specific burst waveform motifs, including atypical hemispheric lateralization and disrupted interhemispheric connectivity. Critically, motif-specific burst rates were systematically related to motor performance across individuals, with similar relations observed across groups. These findings indicate that waveform-specific beta burst dynamics track graded variation in motor performance, rather than mapping selectively onto diagnostic status. Beta burst variability may therefore reflect the functional expression of motor performance, rather than a disorder-specific neural mechanism underlying DCD. This work highlights the importance of beta burst waveform diversity for understanding variability in sensorimotor function across development.
