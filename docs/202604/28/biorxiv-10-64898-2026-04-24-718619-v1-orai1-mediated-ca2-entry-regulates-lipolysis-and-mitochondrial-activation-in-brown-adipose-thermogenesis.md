## 摘要
寒冷诱导的棕色脂肪组织 (BAT) 产热对于维持能量稳态至关重要，但这一过程背后的 Ca2+ 依赖性机制尚不完全清楚。在此，我们确定了钙库操纵的 Ca2+ 进入通路的一个组分 Orai1 是 BAT 产热激活的调节因子。通过使用棕色脂肪细胞特异性 Orai1 敲除小鼠模型，我们证明寒冷暴露与通过非经典机制进行的 Orai1 依赖性 Ca2+ 内流相关。Orai1 缺失导致 cAMP-PKA 信号传导受损，降低了脂肪分解酶和产热基因的表达，并减少了线粒体 Ca2+ 摄取和解偶联。这些缺陷最终导致耐寒性降低、脂质积累和能量消耗减少。从机制上讲，Orai1 促进了 Ca2+ 依赖性的腺苷酸环化酶 3 激活，将细胞膜 Ca2+ 进入与 cAMP 产生联系起来，并促进线粒体重塑和氧化代谢。这些发现支持了 Orai1 在协调棕色脂肪细胞中 Ca2+ 进入脂肪分解和线粒体通路中的关键作用，并突显了其在以能量代谢受损为特征的代谢性疾病中的潜在治疗靶点价值。

## Abstract
Cold-induced thermogenesis in brown adipose tissue (BAT) is essential for maintaining energy homeostasis, yet the Ca2+-dependent mechanisms underlying this process remain incompletely understood. Here, we identify Orai1, a component of the store-operated Ca2+ entry pathway, as a regulator of thermogenic activation in BAT. Using a brown adipocyte-specific Orai1 knockout mouse model, we demonstrate that cold exposure is associated with Orai1-dependent Ca2+ influx through a non-canonical mechanism. Orai1 deficiency leads to impaired cAMP-PKA signaling, reduces the expression of lipolytic enzymes and thermogenic genes, and diminished mitochondrial Ca2+ uptake and uncoupling. These defects culminate in cold intolerance, lipid accumulation, and decreased energy expenditure. Mechanistically, Orai1 facilitates Ca2+-dependent activation of adenylyl cyclase 3, linking membrane Ca2+ entry to cAMP production, and promotes mitochondrial remodeling and oxidative metabolism. These findings support a key role for Orai1 in coordinating Ca2+ entry to lipolytic and mitochondrial pathways in brown adipocytes and highlight its potential therapeutic target in metabolic diseases characterized by impaired energy metabolism.
