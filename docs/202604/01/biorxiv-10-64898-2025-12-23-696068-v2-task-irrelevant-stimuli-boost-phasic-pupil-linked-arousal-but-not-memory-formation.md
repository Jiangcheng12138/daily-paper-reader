## 摘要
包括蓝斑去甲肾上腺素系统在内的脑干唤醒系统会对行为相关事件产生瞬时反应。蓝斑活动还会驱动瞳孔扩张，这在认知任务中经常被观察到。刺激材料编码期间的瞳孔反应强度可以预测其随后的提取成功率，这可能反映了去甲肾上腺素对突触可塑性和记忆形成的影响。瞳孔也会对任务无关的声音产生扩张反应，因此这可以作为研究相位性瞳孔关联唤醒对认知因果影响的有价值工具。在本研究中，我们评估了任务无关的白噪声是否会影响记忆形成和基于记忆的决策。这些声音在记忆材料（图像或口头词汇）呈现之前、期间或之后播放。记忆成功率在次日的再认和自由回忆任务中进行测量。在没有任务无关声音的情况下，单词编码期间瞳孔扩张幅度的逐试次变化预测了记忆成功。任务无关的白噪声也显著地扩张了瞳孔，但并未改善单词或图像的记忆形成。我们得出结论，由任务无关声音触发的瞳孔关联唤醒过程与记忆形成过程中内源性招募的唤醒过程（例如在情绪增强或注意力集中的状态下）有所不同。

## Abstract
AO_SCPLOWBSTRACTC_SCPLOWBrainstem arousal systems including the locus coeruleus noradrenaline system, respond transiently to behaviorally relevant events. Locus coeruleus activity also drives dilations of the pupil, which are often observed during cognitive tasks. The strength of pupil responses during encoding of stimulus material predicts the success of its later retrieval, which might reflect the impact of noradrenaline on synaptic plasticity and memory formation. The pupil also dilates in response to task-irrelevant sounds, which could therefore serve as a valuable tool for investigating causal effects of phasic, pupil-linked arousal on cognition. Here, we evaluated whether task-irrelevant white noise sounds affect memory formation and memory-based decisions. These sounds were played before, during or after the presentation of memoranda (images or spoken words). Memory success was measured in recognition and free recall tasks the day after. Trial-to-trial variations in the amplitude of pupil dilations during word encoding without task-irrelevant sounds predicted memory success. Task-irrelevant white-noise sounds also robustly dilated the pupil but did not improve memory formation for the words or the images. We conclude that pupil-linked arousal processes triggered by task-irrelevant sounds differ from those recruited endogenously during memory formation, for example in states of increased emotionality or attention.
