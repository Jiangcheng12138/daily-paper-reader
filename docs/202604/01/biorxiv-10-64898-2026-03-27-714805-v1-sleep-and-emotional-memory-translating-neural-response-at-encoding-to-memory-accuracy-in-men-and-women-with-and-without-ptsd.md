## 摘要
背景：越来越多的证据表明，睡眠在创伤后应激障碍（PTSD）的预后中发挥着重要作用，这可能归因于其对情绪记忆巩固的影响，但相关机制尚不明确。本研究旨在检验以下假设：睡眠神经生理学、PTSD 状态和性别调节了晚期正电位（LPP）中介情感视觉刺激记忆准确性的程度。方法：39 名参与者（18 名女性）在记录脑电图（EEG）的同时观看了 75 张负面和 75 张中性 IAPS 图像。观看图像后，参与者进行了两小时的午睡，随后进行了记忆评估。记忆准确性通过 d' = Z(命中率) - Z(虚报率) 进行衡量，其中命中率是指在记忆评估中被正确识别为此前见过的图像比例，虚报率是指在记忆评估中被错误识别为此前见过的图像比例，Z() 是标准正态分布函数的逆累积分布函数。结果：与中性刺激相比，早期（300-1000 毫秒）和晚期（1000-1500 毫秒）LPP 中介了情绪刺激辨别准确性（d'）的提升（ps < 0.001）。晚期 LPP 与 d' 之间的关联受睡眠调节，表现为当参与者在 N3 和 REM 睡眠阶段所占时间比例较高时，该关联更强（p = 0.02）。与对照组相比，PTSD+ 个体在早期和晚期 LPP 对情绪与中性图像的反应性差异均有所减弱（ps < 0.001）。尽管中介分析结果显示情绪刺激的 d' 高于中性刺激，但女性对负面刺激的记忆准确性总体上低于中性刺激（p < 0.001），而男性则无显著差异（p = 0.64）。结论：N3 和 REM 睡眠对于产生强烈且持续神经反应的刺激记忆至关重要。PTSD 的特征是区分负面与中性信息的能力下降。开展更多研究以理解性别对情绪记忆的影响至关重要。

## Abstract
Background: Growing evidence suggests that sleep plays an important role in PTSD outcomes, potentially due to its influence on emotional memory consolidation, though these mechanisms remain unknown. This study sought to test the hypotheses that sleep neurophysiology, PTSD status, and sex moderates the degree to which the late positive potential (LPP) mediates memory accuracy for affective visual stimuli. Methods: N = 39 participants (18 female) viewed 75 negative and 75 neutral IAPS images while EEG was recorded. After viewing the images, participants took a two-hour long nap which was followed by a memory assessment. Memory accuracy was measured using d' = Z(hit rate) - Z(false alarm rate), where hit rate refers to the proportion of images seen during the memory assessment that are correctly identified as being previously seen, false alarm rate refers to the proportion of images seen during the memory assessment that are incorrectly identified as being previously seen, and Z() is the inverse cumulative distribution function of the standard normal distribution function. Results: The early (300 -- 1000 ms) and late (1000 -- 1500 ms) LPP mediated enhanced discrimination accuracy for emotional compared to neural stimuli (d') (ps < 0.001). The association between the late LPP and d' was moderated by sleep such that the association was stronger when participants spent proportionately more time in N3 and REM (p = 0.02). The differences in reactivity between emotional and neutral images for both the early and late LPP were attenuated in PTSD+ individuals vs. controls (ps < 0.001). Despite mediation results showing greater d' for emotional compared to neutral stimuli, women showed overall worse memory accuracy for negative compared to neutral stimuli (p < 0.001) whereas men showed no difference (p = 0.64). Conclusions: N3 and REM sleep play a critical role for memory of stimuli that produce large and sustained neural responses. PTSD is marked by a diminished ability to distinguish between negative and neutral information. More research is critical to understand sex effects on emotional memory.

*   **精细的时间窗口分析**：区分了早期和晚期 LPP，发现只有晚期持续的神经反应才依赖睡眠进行巩固，这为“标签-巩固”理论提供了新证据。

*   **多维度整合**：将神经生理（ERP）、睡眠生理（PSG）与临床状态（PTSD）及性别差异整合在一个统一的中介模型中。

#### 8. 不足与局限
*   **样本量限制**：N=39 的样本量在进行多重交互作用分析（如性别 × PTSD × 睡眠）时统计效能可能不足。
*   **午睡范式**：研究基于白天午睡，REM 睡眠时间较短（平均 10 分钟），可能无法完全模拟整夜睡眠对情绪记忆的巩固作用。
*   **因果关系**：虽然使用了中介模型，但本质上仍是相关性研究，无法绝对断定睡眠结构改变是记忆差异的唯一原因。
*   **刺激强度**：实验使用的负面图片可能不足以诱发某些受试者（尤其是女性）产生预期的情绪增强效应，甚至可能引发了回避行为。
