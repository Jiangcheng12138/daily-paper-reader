## 摘要
在单细胞分辨率下将分子身份与体内功能联系起来，仍然是神经科学领域的一项重大挑战。在本研究中，我们通过结合细胞分辨率的双光子成像和空间转录组学的端到端流程，填补了小鼠海马体研究中的这一空白。在虚拟现实导航任务中表现出异质生理反应的 CA1 中间神经元，通过基因表达被事后聚类为 5 个 GABA 能亚类和 14 个类型。单个细胞的生理反应与转录组轴相一致，且仅基于生理特征训练的分类器也还原了相同的有序结构。我们的方法建立了一个直接且可扩展的框架，用于将体内回路动力学与组成细胞的身份联系起来，揭示了一个涵盖海马抑制性神经元结构和功能多样性的转录组轴。一句话总结：通过从行为到空间转录组学追踪神经元，将海马体的体内功能与其分子身份联系起来。

## Abstract
Linking molecular identity to function in vivo at single-cell resolution remains an outstanding challenge in neuroscience. Here, we bridge this gap in the mouse hippocampus with an end-to-end pipeline of cell-resolved two-photon imaging and spatial transcriptomics. CA1 interneurons exhibiting heterogeneous physiological responses during a virtual-reality navigation task were post hoc clustered by gene expression into 5 GABAergic subclasses and 14 types. Physiological responses of individual cells aligned with a transcriptomic axis, and a classifier trained on physiological features alone recovered the same ordered organization. Our approach establishes a direct, scalable framework for linking in vivo circuit dynamics to constituent cell identity, revealing a transcriptomic axis that encompasses the structural and functional diversity of hippocampal inhibitory neurons.
