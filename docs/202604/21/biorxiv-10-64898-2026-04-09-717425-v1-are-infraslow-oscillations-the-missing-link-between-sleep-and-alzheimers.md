## 摘要
引言：蓝斑和类淋巴系统功能障碍均与阿尔茨海默病（AD）有关，且近期研究发现其与睡眠纺锤波（sigma）活动的超慢波振荡（ISO）相关。本文假设 ISO 的完整性是连接睡眠与 AD 的关键环节。方法：我们分析了 AD 患者和对照组的非快速眼动睡眠脑电图，从 sigma 功率时间序列中提取了 ISO 峰值振幅、固有频率和带宽。我们评估了组间差异以及其与血浆生物标志物（Aβ42/40、pTau181 和 217、NfL、GFAP）的相关性。结果：AD 患者的 ISO 峰值振幅显著降低，而固有频率和带宽保持不变。ISO 峰值振幅与 Aβ42/40 比值呈正相关，ISO 带宽与 GFAP 和 NfL 水平以及较低的言语记忆保持力相关。讨论：AD 中 ISO 的这种选择性减弱与蓝斑功能障碍和类淋巴循环受损一致。ISO 可能是一种将睡眠微结构与 AD 病理联系起来的新型机制和电生理标志物。

## Abstract
INTRODUCTIONLocus coeruleus and glymphatic dysfunction are linked both to Alzheimers disease (AD) and, recently, to infraslow oscillation in sleep spindle (sigma) activity (ISO). Here we hypothesise ISO integrity is a critical link between sleep and AD.

METHODSWe analyzed non-rapid eye movement sleep EEG from AD and controls, extracting ISO peak amplitude, intrinsic frequency, and bandwidth from the sigma-power time course. We assessed group differences and correlations with plasma biomarkers (A{beta}42/40, pTau181 and 217, NfL, GFAP).

RESULTSISO peak amplitude was significantly reduced in AD, while intrinsic frequency and bandwidth were preserved. ISO peak amplitude positively correlated with A{beta}42/40 ratio, and ISO bandwidth correlated with GFAP and NfL levels, and with lower verbal memory retention.

DISCUSSIONSuch selective weakening of ISO in AD is consistent with LC dysfunction and impaired glymphatic cycling. ISO may be a novel mechanism and electrophysiological marker linking sleep microarchitecture to AD pathology.

*   **机制连接**：成功将电生理信号（EEG）与分子病理（血浆标志物）及临床表现（记忆）联系起来。
*   **应用潜力**：ISO 特征可由家用便携 EEG 设备提取，具备作为低成本、非侵入性 AD 筛查标志物的潜力。

*   **因果关系不明**：横断面研究无法确定是 ISO 受损导致了病理堆积，还是病理损伤了产生 ISO 的神经环路。
