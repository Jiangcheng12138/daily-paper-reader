## 摘要
哺乳动物肝脏代谢在禁食期间会发生重大转变。热力学原理对代谢施加了基本约束，而反应的吉布斯自由能变化（ΔrG'）指示了反应的方向以及与平衡态的距离。然而，完整哺乳动物器官中的 ΔrG' 景观在很大程度上仍未被表征。在本研究中，我们利用实验测量的绝对代谢物浓度和新开发的计算方法 GLEAM，绘制了禁食期间小鼠肝脏葡萄糖代谢的 ΔrG' 谱图。我们发现，尽管禁食期间代谢物波动剧烈，但相应的 ΔrG' 仍保持稳健，即使是对于在糖酵解和糖异生之间反转方向的反应也是如此。在禁食期间维持限速步骤的潜在候选反应方面，发现了显著的热力学稳健性。这种稳健性是通过消耗大量的酶表达成本实现的，有助于从糖酵解到糖异生的有效切换。此外，尽管存在肥胖引起的代谢紊乱，肥胖小鼠的肝脏也表现出了热力学稳健性。我们的框架为完整器官代谢提供了一个全新的热力学视角，证明了肝脏通过缓冲代谢物浓度差异，稳健地维持了有利于代谢控制的热力学特征。

## Abstract
Mammalian liver metabolism undergoes a substantial shift during fasting. Thermodynamic principles impose fundamental constraints on metabolism, and the Gibbs free energy change of reaction ({Delta}rG') indicates the reactions direction and distance from equilibrium. However, {Delta}rG' landscapes in intact mammalian organs remain largely uncharacterized. Here, we mapped {Delta}rG' profile of glucose metabolism in mouse liver during fasting, using experimentally measured absolute metabolite concentrations and a newly developed computational method, GLEAM. We found that despite large metabolite fluctuations during fasting, the corresponding {Delta}rG's remained robust, even for reactions reversing the direction between glycolysis and gluconeogenesis. A remarkable thermodynamic robustness is found in maintaining potential candidates of rate-limiting steps during fasting. This robustness is achieved by expending substantial costs for enzyme expressions, contributing to efficient switching from glycolysis to gluconeogenesis. Furthermore, obese mouse liver also showed thermodynamic robustness despite obesity-induced metabolic disruption. Our framework provided a novel thermodynamic perspective on intact organ metabolism, demonstrating that the liver robustly maintains thermodynamic characteristics favorable for metabolic control by buffering metabolite concentration differences.
